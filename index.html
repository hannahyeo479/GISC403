Bucket List Walks in New Zealand
Presented in this web map is a collection of walks throughout New Zealand that are on my bucket list, symbolised by point markers that have their location georeferenced by the coordinates of the mountain peaks to the base map. There is also a rectangular polygon to visualise the area covered by the Milford Track as well as a thick red line to symbolise the entire route. The thick bright blue polygon border was chosen to distinguish it from the Territory Authority polygons which are the thinner green boundary lines.
